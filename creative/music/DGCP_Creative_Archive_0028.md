Title:
Under the Same Sun

Framework:

## Intro

Sun was high

No place to run

---

## Verse 1

Dust was dry air stood still

Heat came down across the field

Dogs were close no space to move

Something changed in how they moved

One small spark one wrong turn

Under heat the tempers burn

Didn’t mean to go that far

But the sun was hard

---

## Pre-Chorus

No one spoke no one knew

What the heat was leading to

---

## Chorus

Under the same sun

We were standing

Some stayed strong

One stopped breathing

No one planned

How it would go

Just the heat

And the flow

Under the same sun

Nothing loud

Just a silence

Over the ground

---

## Verse 2

Shadows short no escape

Every breath was getting late

Some stepped back some stayed in

Some just did not know where to begin

Now it is calm now it is still

Same old field same old hill

But one space feels different now

Even without a sound
