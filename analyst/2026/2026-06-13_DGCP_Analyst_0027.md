### System Context

Electricity remains one of the most essential components of modern civilization.

Homes require electricity.

Businesses require electricity.

Hospitals require electricity.

Industries require electricity.

Digital systems require electricity.

Many observers focus on power generation, utility bills, energy prices, or electrical equipment.

The discussion frequently centers on electricity itself.

The systems enabled by electricity receive less attention.

While reviewing developments across industry, healthcare, communications, transportation, and digital infrastructure, I found myself paying less attention to electricity as a utility service and more attention to the systems operating through it.

The electricity remained visible.

The infrastructure became visible.

The observation was not about energy alone.

The observation was about dependency.
